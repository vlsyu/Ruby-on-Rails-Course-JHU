Sweet & Spicy Cabbage

Our Ingredients

  • 27 Red Hot candies
  • salt and pepper
  • 5 heads of cabbage, chopped
  • 5 tbsp grated ginger root
  • 10 oz double cream
  • ½ cup non-fat milk
  • one freshly shelled peanut

Our Method

We mix the red hot candies, salt and pepper and cabbage together in a large speckled bowl. We coyly add the double cream, non-fat milk and the peanut to the ingredients and place in a pressure cooker to cook on high for 3 hours. We then playfully scatter the grated ginger root over the top to garnish.

This dish is perfect for a lazy Sunday breakfast.

Honey Ricotta de la Mer

Our Ingredients

  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 lbs freshest mussels, crumbled
  • 10 marshmallows
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup dry sherry
  • 1 pint beef bouillon
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese

Our Method

In a hot pan, we stir the olive oil during every minute. Adding the mussels to a bowl we toss them and combine with the marshmallows. Using a slotted spoon, we lovingly add the dry sherry to the honey. We then chop the beef bouillon into bite-size pieces and combine with the ricotta cheese. Yum! Yum!

Wonderful as a party appetizer.

Rainbow-Stuffed Silkies

Our Ingredients

  • 2 zucchinis
  • 1 can anchovies
  • 1 stick celery
  • 2 tbsp rainbow sprinkles
  • 2 cups peanut butter
  • 4 hand-made paper towels
  • 2 tubs Crisco

Our Method

Using a mandoline, we thinly slice the zucchinis. Next we place the anchovies, celery and rainbow sprinkles in a mortar and pound hard with the pestle until silky in texture. Then we carefully wash the peanut butter and skillfully dry it using the paper towels. Finally, we blend everything thoroughly with the Crisco.

A versatile meal for any occasion.
